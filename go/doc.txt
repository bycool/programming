1 go开发环境搭建

系统：rhel6.9
go包: go1.12.5.linux-amd64.tar.gz

tar xvf go1.12.5.linux-amd64.tar.gz -C /usr/local

export PATH=$PATH:/usr/local/go/bin

运行 go
[ten@localhost datum]$ go
Go is a tool for managing Go source code.

Usage:

	go <command> [arguments]
    ...

环境搭建成功。
